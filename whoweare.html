• Alonili Press

    • Who We Are

    Alonili Press is the unconventional publishing company founded by Katherine Munson in 2020 and is named after the similarly-unconventional kingdom in her Eye of the Master series. It is inspired by Pixar's Braintrust and built off of the concept that artists, like anyone else, thrive best in community. Instead of a traditional submission-based company, Alonili Press is connected to Alonili Council, a community of authors dedicated to building one another up both as artists and as people, and to pushing each others' stories to reach their full potential.
