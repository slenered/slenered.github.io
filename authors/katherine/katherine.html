    Katherine Munson is an Oregon author who began writing stories at four years old. Her very first story was inspired by an ugly hand-me-down doll, Grumpy Gorana, who became the sympathetic villain in a series of superhero books. Ever since then, Katherine has been unable to stop telling stories, whether on the page or through other art forms such as music, dance, or theatre. Today, among many other projects, she continues to write and rewrite the books in her Eye of the Master series, which she started in 2010. Other facts about Katherine include that she is the oldest of six siblings, one of whom is also a writer. She is the proud owner of a beautiful python named Ariadne, and even more proudly the wife of her sweet and goofy husband, Garrett. She graduated from Corban University in 2019 with a double major in Creative Writing and General Psychology.
